Weather Policy
If the weather is questionable, we'll try and make a decision as soon as we can each Thursday and will notify everyone via email and this web site. Tunxis' policy is that unless there is lightning or it's a major downpour, expect to play.
If, while we are on the course, a storm develops that is obviously not a passing shower, we can end play. If the first group has finished 9 holes, the round will be counted for the entire league. All remaining hole points will be split between the teams for any teams that have not completed 9 holes and low net scores will be calculated based on the number of completed holes.
