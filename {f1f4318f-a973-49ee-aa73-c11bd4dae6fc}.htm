Sub Policy

 

 

  • All players are responsible for getting their own sub if they are unable to golf any week. All subs pay a fee of $10 per round.

  • It is recommended that a sub is used from the sub list since most of them have handicaps in our league but anyone can sub for you, if necessary.

 

 

Sub Scoring

 

 

  • All points that are earned by the sub, both individually and team, are awarded to the missing player. Note: Subs are handicapped at a slightly lower handicap.

  • If a player cannot get a sub for a given week, a "ghost" sub will used for the round.

 

Ghost Subs

 

 

  • Ghost subs are used to simulate a substitution.

  • The ghost sub is automatically given a score equal to your handicapped score + 5 strokes (penalty). For instance, if your handicap is a 10, then your typical score for 36 holes would be 46. The ghost sub is given a score of 51.

  • Scoring is done the same way with the ghost sub.
