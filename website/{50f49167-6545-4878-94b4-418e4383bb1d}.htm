There are 36 points awarded in each weekly match.

 

  1. 18 points - Individual Head-To-Head Per Hole - The A player from each team and the B player from each team play head to head (with handicap strokes factored in) and are awarded 1 point per hole. Ties award 1/2 point to each player.

 

  1. 9 points - Team Score Per Hole - Team totals are also added each hole (with handicap strokes factored in) and teams are awarded 1 point per hole for 9 more points.

 

  1. 9 points - Low Net - The final 9 points are awarded for low net. 3 points are awarded for low net for each individual match and 3 more points are awarded for team low net.
