Tardiness Policy
Our tee times begin at 4:20 but please be there at least 10 minutes before your tee time.
If you are not available at your assigned tee time, the next available foursome should tee off. If no other foursomes are available, the players that are there should begin play and the missing player will have to catch up to the group (but the missing player will get the max score for the holes he missed).
