Week 9 | Letting Go

This Week

  1. NOTE: Through the Week 10 lesson, you unfortunately will have some older versions of the lecture videos. Leslie and John are hard at work to complete the last few new ones. You have lifetime access to the curriculum so you will be able to view them when they are ready and posted, whenever that may be.
  2. Watch the videos for Week 9.
  3. Read Week 9 in your workbook and answer questions.
  4. Attend the weekly call.




This Week's Discussion Questions

  1. Share something from the chapter that got your attention.
  2. In general, what makes it hard to “let go” of something?
  3. What things are you having to let go of in the context of your marriage right now?
  4. Letting go of everything that has happened and leaving the past behind is a process. Where do you think you are in that process?
  5. What makes it hard to embrace the ‘new or the unknown’?
  6. Do you believe that God desires good for you and that He can replace with good things that which you have lost?
  7. How are you attempting to let go of your mate’s recovery?
  8. What should we do with fear and anxiety?
  9. What did you take away from this chapter?
