Week 5 | Brokenness
 

What to do this week:

  1. Watch the video “Brokenness”.
  2. Read the AR note titled “Pride verses Brokenness”.
  3. Now (for the last time) read the story you shared in Week Two. Was the person writing this story broken or proud?
  4. Be prepared to share how you see yourself on the continuum between pride and brokenness.
