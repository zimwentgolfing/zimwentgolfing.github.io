How Position Week and Playoffs Works
There will be a first half and a second half winner. Two position weeks will be scheduled at the end of each half. The final week of the season will be a playoff where the first place team of each half. The winner of that match will be crowned the Overall League Champion and the loser will be the Second Place League winner. Similarly, the second place teams from each half will also playoff to battle for the overall third place winner for the season. Note that prizes are only given for the first 3 teams. Each team can only win 1 prize so if the same team wins both halves, they DO NOT win the first place and second place prizes.
 
 
 
 
 
Two position weeks are scheduled for the season, one at the middle of the season and one at the end. Teams are ordered using the following criteria. This is the same criteria to determine standings.
 
1) Total Number of Points (Individual and Team)
2) Total Number of Team Points
 
- Tiebreakers will be decided by the number of points won by each tied team against all other tied teams.
